Become a Member

Join the Canada Pansori Center as a supporting member

How to Register

To become a member, please send an email to torontopansori@gmail.com with your full name, phone number, and the subject line “Membership Registration”.

Annual membership fee: $50 CAD

You can send your membership fee via e-Transfer to the same email address.

Membership Benefits

  • Priority access to workshops, lectures, and events
  • Discounted tickets for performances
  • Opportunities to participate in exclusive members-only programs
  • Newsletter with updates and behind-the-scenes stories
  • Support the preservation and promotion of Korean traditional music in Canada

Questions?

If you have any questions about membership, feel free to reach out by email or through our Contact Us page.
