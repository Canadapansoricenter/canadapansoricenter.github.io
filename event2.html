Event Information

SORI25 is a two-part program that invites participants of all backgrounds to explore Korean music and create meaningful sounds together.

Part 1: Pansori Workshop
Discover the expressive world of pansori, minyo (folk songs), and other Korean vocal forms.

When: Wednesdays, 6–8 PM | July–September 2025
Where: North York Central Library (near North York Station)

Part 2: Rehearsal & Concert
After the workshop series, we’ll move into rehearsals to prepare for a special participant concert in November.

When: October (Rehearsals) & November (Concert)
Where: Location TBD

This program is proudly supported by the Toronto Arts Council, allowing us to offer accessible pricing:

  • $60 for 2025 Canada Pansori Center members
  • $120 for non-members
